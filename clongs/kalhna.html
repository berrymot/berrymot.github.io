phonotactics

syllable structure: (C)(C)V(V)(C)(C)

double letters are pronounced with a glottal stop or /h/ in between.

disallowed consonant clusters

onset:

  • a nasal followed by /p/
  • two consecutive plosives
  • /sz/ or /zs/

coda:

  • consonant followed by /ʔ/ or /w/
  • <td, dt, kg, gk, sz, zs, cr, rc>
